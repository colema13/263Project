Surveillance technologies continue to expand across public and private spaces. This project explores the implications of facial recognition, digital monitoring, and modern systems of control.

About Us

Our project explores the powers of facial recognition technology and the dangers associated with it. While it can be used for good, many do not care about the average person and the associated risks that have come with the implementation of the technology. Our goal is to inform people of the injustices taking place throughout the United States bad practices that take place not only from the government, but also the companies themselves. By informing people on these bad practices, we hope to create discussion and eventually laws that provide safer methods of tracking.
